Summer Watermelon Gazpacho

This twist on classic gazpacho will be a welcome, refreshing sight on those hot summer days!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Place the watermelon, cucumber, red bell pepper, mint, basil, cilantro, and red onion into a blender.
  2. Pour in the red wine vinegar and olive oil.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.
