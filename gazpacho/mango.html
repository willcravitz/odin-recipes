Mango Gazpacho

Serve this intriguing variation of gazpacho at your next summer gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped mango
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup sherry vinegar
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine mangos, onion, cucumber, bell pepper, olive oil, sherry vinegar, parsley, garlic, oregano, cumin, salt, and black pepper in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate until chilled, at least 1 hour.
